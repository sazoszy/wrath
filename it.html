Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT)

The Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) program of the University of Caloocan City is designed to equip students with practical skills and technical knowledge in computing technologies used in modern organizations. The program emphasizes hands-on experience, problem-solving, and real-world IT applications.

Program Focus

The BSIT program focuses on the implementation, administration, and management of information technology solutions to support business operations and organizational goals.

Key Areas of Study

• Network Administration
• Web and Mobile Development
• Cybersecurity Fundamentals
• Database Management
• Systems Analysis and Design

Skills You Will Gain

Students develop skills in troubleshooting, system configuration, programming, teamwork, and effective communication, preparing them for industry-ready roles.

Career Opportunities

Graduates may pursue careers such as:
• IT Support Specialist
• Network Administrator
• Web Developer
• Systems Technician
• Junior Cybersecurity Analyst
