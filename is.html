Bachelor of Science in Information Systems (BSIS)

The Bachelor of Science in Information Systems (BSIS) program integrates business processes and information technology to support organizational decision-making and operations.

Program Focus

Focuses on analyzing business requirements and designing technology-based solutions that improve efficiency.

Key Areas of Study

• Systems Analysis & Design
• Business Intelligence
• Enterprise Systems
• Database Systems
• Project Management

Skills You Will Gain

Business analysis, system documentation, communication, project coordination, and data-driven decision-making.

Career Opportunities

• Systems Analyst
• Business Analyst
• IT Project Coordinator
• ERP Specialist
• Data Analyst (Junior)
