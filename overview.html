Program Overview

Information Technology (IT)

Focuses on software development, networking, system administration, and IT support. Prepares students for careers in managing technology and digital infrastructures.

Computer Science (CS)

Emphasizes programming, algorithms, AI, and software engineering. Equips students for careers in software development, research, and innovation.

Information Systems (IS)

Focuses on using technology to support business processes, data management, and decision-making. Prepares students for roles in system analysis and business technology integration.

Entertainment and Multimedia Computing (EMC)

Combines technology and creativity for multimedia production, game development, animation, and digital content creation. Prepares students for careers in the digital entertainment industry.
