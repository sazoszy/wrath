Bachelor of Science in Entertainment and Multimedia Computing (EMC)

The Entertainment and Multimedia Computing (EMC) program combines creativity and technology, focusing on digital media, game development, animation, and interactive applications.

Program Focus

Focuses on multimedia production, game design, animation, and interactive digital experiences.

Key Areas of Study

• Game Development
• Digital Animation
• Multimedia Design
• UI / UX Design
• Interactive Media

Skills You Will Gain

Creative design, visual storytelling, multimedia programming, and user experience development.

Career Opportunities

• Game Developer
• Multimedia Artist
• UI/UX Designer
• Animation Assistant
• Digital Content Creator
