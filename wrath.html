Welcome to the Computer Science Department

The Computer Science Department of the University of Caloocan City is committed to excellence in computing education, research, and innovation. The department prepares students for careers in technology, software development, and digital transformation.

About the Department

The Computer Science Department of the University of Caloocan City aims to produce competent, innovative, and socially responsible computing professionals. The department emphasizes both theoretical foundations and practical skills to meet the demands of the rapidly evolving technology industry.

Why Choose UCC – Computer Science?

Industry-Relevant Curriculum

Programs are designed to align with current industry standards, emerging technologies, and real-world applications.

Experienced Faculty

Learn from qualified instructors with academic expertise and professional industry experience.

Hands-on Learning

Students gain practical experience through projects, laboratories, and collaborative activities.

Career-Oriented Programs

Graduates are prepared for employment, certifications, and further studies in computing and technology.

Vision and Mission

Vision

A competitive and upright university committed to building a WISE (World-class, Innovative, Sustainable, Empowered) community.

Mission

To provide quality instruction, impactful co-curricular activities, sustainable community engagement, and research & development initiatives through a dynamic quality management system and learner empowerment to become agents of holistic change.
